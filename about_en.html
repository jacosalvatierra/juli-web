About me

As an architect, I have always been fascinated by space.

Just like built architecture, physical space requires grounding (foundation) and unfolding (movement, rhythm, light).

In my yoga classes, I work extensively with these visual concepts to strengthen imagination, joy of life, and individual self-expression. Time and again while teaching, I experience how a warm hand placed during an adjustment has a far deeper impact than any verbal explanation.

This realization made me curious to learn more about touch and brought me to Thai Yoga Massage trainings in Greece, Berlin, France, and Buenos Aires.

Incorporating Osteothai massage expanded my horizon immensely, allowing me to integrate and stimulate internal organs within this gentle yet powerful bodywork practice.

I look forward to connecting with you in German, English, and Spanish.

I believe the journey continues, and I am excited to meet you on the yoga mat or on the peaceful oasis of my massage futon.
