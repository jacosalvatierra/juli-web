Vinyasa Yoga

Together we flow through dynamic and grounding yoga sequences, holding and aligning asanas while exploring dancer-like wild forms of expression.

The breath is always the oil that softens our power and makes our devotion active.

Theme-based, such as backbends, forward folds, inversions, or chakras, I draw from all yoga styles (Vinyasa, Yin, or Liquid). Everything is welcome, nothing is forced!

My yoga classes are based on the possible, not the impossible—falling, flying, and trying again.

Interested in a small individual group? Or would you like to deepen or start your practice in personal 1-on-1 sessions? Feel free to get in touch—we can arrange this in the studios in Leipzig and Bielefeld. I am also fluent in English and Spanish.

„Come to the mat to feel, not to accomplish.
Shift your focus and your heart will open"
