GIFT A VOUCHER

Thai Yoga Massage is meditation and dance through touch.

Expect both a deeply relaxing experience on a physical and emotional level, as well as an invigorating, stretched feeling as after a yoga practice, only lying down.

The Thai Yoga Massage takes place fully clothed on a futon on the floor, while the receiver rests in complete relaxation.

Through gentle rocking, swaying, and pressure on the body, spaces are rhythmically created between movement and rest, activating energy channels and releasing blockages in muscles, tissue, and skin structures.
